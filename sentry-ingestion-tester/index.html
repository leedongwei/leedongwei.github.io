Sentry.io JS Ingestion Tester

  • Send lots of JavaScript traffic to a Sentry project!
  • You will need to turn off adblockers for this to work.
  • If you are using a paid account on production, this will cost real money!
  • Open the JS console (Cmd + Option + J on Chrome).
  • Events are sent synchronously with 200ms delay between them.
  • It may take up to a minute for traffic to show up on the Sentry UI.
